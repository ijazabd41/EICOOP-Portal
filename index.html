About us

Emirati Industries Cooperative — established to support food security, local industry and shareholder value.

The official name is Consumer Cooperative for the UAE Food Industries, and the trade name is Emirati Industries Cooperative.

Emirati Industries Cooperative — officially registered as the Consumer Cooperative for the UAE Food Industries — is a citizen-owned industrial and commercial cooperative established in alignment with the UAE's Principles of the 50, introduced by His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum, Vice President and Prime Minister of the UAE and Ruler of Dubai. In direct support of the Eighth Principle, which champions citizen-owned cooperative enterprises in strategic sectors such as food security, healthcare, and education, our Cooperative was founded to translate this national vision into tangible economic value for UAE citizens and residents alike.

We operate at the heart of the UAE's essential food industry, with a focus on rice, grains, spices, and other packaged food products. Our strategy centers on establishing a dedicated food packaging and processing facility, investing in grain cultivation and agricultural production, and building a robust wholesale and retail distribution network — spanning company-owned outlets and select third-party retailers — to deliver high-quality food products at competitive prices.

Our mission is simple yet significant: to reduce living costs, improve access to essential food products, and strengthen the UAE's national self-sufficiency in food security. Beyond commercial performance, we are committed to generating long-term value for our shareholders, creating employment opportunities for UAE Nationals, and introducing innovative, technology-driven practices across food manufacturing and retail.

Operating under the supervision of the UAE Ministry of Economy, Emirati Industries Cooperative is structured for sustainable growth — with founding shareholders holding a 10.25% equity stake and the remaining 89.75% of share capital open to public subscription, inviting broader citizen participation in this national economic initiative.

Our purpose — in simple words

To make essential food more affordable and support the UAE's future.

Emirati Industries Cooperative was started to reduce living costs, provide good-quality essential food at fair prices, support UAE food security and create long-term value for shareholders. The Cooperative plans to manufacture, package and supply food through wholesale channels, its own stores and selected retail partners. It also aims to support local agriculture, create jobs for UAE Nationals and use modern technology in food manufacturing and retail.
